For my final assignment I have created an interactive ps.5 art piece titled 'Dream Archive' that reflects the concepts of memory, dreaming, and my personal relationship to remembering. The "memory fragments" that I included as collectibles are scanned 35mm photographs that I took personally over a span of several years. Some of the photographs are in-camera multiple exporsures, which ties back to the dream-like atmosphere. <p> 

I used keyboard movement, arrays, conditionals, image assets, and simple animation to build the interaction. I also used Gen AI as a support tool for troubleshooting and structuring parts of the code.
